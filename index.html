Do you wish to become a writer?

English for Writers is an online dictionary in a form of flashcards that is composed of the most common words found in the greatest books of the literature. The words are randomly picked from the books of the authors including J.K.Rowling, Oscar Wilde, Stephen King, Ernest Hemingway, and many others American and British writers. The goal of this platform is to learn the style and words commonly used in the standard English language of literature. English for Writers can be useful for both native speakers of English and for foreign individuals who would like to start writing books in the English language.
